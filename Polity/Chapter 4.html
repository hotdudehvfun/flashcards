Preamble
First preamble:American

Based on Objective Resolution by Nehru 1946, adopted by assembly on 22 jan 1947
Preamble has been amended only ONCE so far
  1. 42nd  Amendment 1976
  2. added three words Socialist, Secular and Integrity to the Preamble

INGREDIENTS OF THE PREAMBLE
1. Source of authority: people of India
2. Nature of Indian State: sovereign, socialist, secular democratic and republican polity
3. Objective of the Constitution: justice, liberty, equality and fraternity as the objectives
4. Date of adoption: November 26, 1949

Features
  1. Sovereign
    1. free from outside interference
  2. Socialist
    1. explicit keyword added by 42nd amendment 1976
    2. but socialist content had already been in DPSP
    3. India: democratic socialism(mixed economy), not a communistic socialism, which involved nationalisation of all means
    4. mix of marxism and gandhism socialism
    5. 1991:LPG policy diluted the socialist credentials of indian state
  3. Secular
    1. word added by 42nd amendment 1976
    2. btu even before that Articles: Religion: 25-28 show secularism.
    3. India: positive secularism: all religions are equal
  4. Democratic
    1. India: based on popular sovereignty.
    2. we have indirect democracy: Represntative democracy
    3. Direct democracy: referendum(direct votes), initiative(people can propose bills to legislation), recall(people can remove officers), plebiscite( obtaining people's opinion)
    4. India: democracy in political, social and economical
    5. Egalitarian: all are equal
  5. Republic
    1. Head of state elected direct or indirect
    2. Vesting political sovereignty in the people and not in single person
  6. Justice
    1. social, economic and political has been taken from the Russian Revolution (1917)
  7. Liberty
    1. Liberty, equality and fraternity in our Preamble have been taken from the French Revolution (1789-1799)
  8. Equality
    1. FRs:Articles 15,16,17,18
    2. Article 325: no person to be declared ineligible on grounds of sex, caste, race for electoral rolls
    3. DPSC Article 39: equal pay equal work
  9. Fraternity
    1. Brotherhood also in Fundamental Duties Article: 51-A
    2. integrity: added by 42nd Act 1976


PREAMBLE AS PART OF THE CONSTITUTION
1. Berubari Union case 1960
SC it is not part of Constitution

2. Kesavananda Bharati case (1973)
  1. SC said it is part of Constitution
  2. Preamble can be amended, subject to the condition that no amendment is done to basic features
3. LIC of India Case 1995
  1. SC again held preamble is a part of constitution
Preamble was inserted at the END.
Preamble is not neither a source of power nor prohibition for legislature
Non-Justiciable in nature but all the key ideas of preamble have been included in various article of constitution.
