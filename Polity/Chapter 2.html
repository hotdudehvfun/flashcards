Making of the Constitution


  1. 1934 Idea of constitution put forward by M.N Roy
  2. 1935 INC for the first time, officially demanded a Constituent Assembly
  3. 1938 Jawaharlal Nehru, on behalf the INC declared that Constitution of free India must be framed, without outside interference, by a Constituent Assembly elected on the basis of adult franchise
  4. 1940 August Offer accepted the demand of constituent assembly
  5. 1942 Cripps Mission: formation of responsible govt after the war, rejected by muslim league and congress
  6. 1942: Non-Cooperation Movement, similar to revolt of 1857
  7. Cabinet came to India with a draft proposal of the British Government on the framing of an independent Constitution to be adopted after the World War II
  8. Rejected by both ML and INC
  9. 1946 Cabinet Mission Plan
  10. Formed Constituent Assembly in Nov. 1946
  11. Seats allocation: Muslims, Sikhs, General
  12. Indirect election: single transferable vote
  13. Partially elected partially nominated
  14. 1 seat per million
  15. princely states were not filled as they decided to stay away from the Constituent Assembly


WORKING OF THE CONSTITUENT ASSEMBLY
  1. Working
  2. Dr Sachchidanand Sinha temporary President
  3. Later Dr Rajendra Prasad
  4. Two Vice Presidents :H.C. Mukherjee and V.T. Krishnamachari V-Ps
  5. First meeting: December 9, 1946
  6. Muslim League boycotted the meeting and insisted on a separate state of Pakistan
  7. Objective Resolution: later became preamble
  8. Moved by Nehru : Dec 1946
  9. India as Independent Sovereign Republic
  10. India shall be union of states
  11. power and authority of the Sovereign Independent India derived from the people
  12. adopted by the Assembly on January 22, 1947
  13. Sanctioned the India's membership of the Commonwealth in May 1949
  14. National flag: July 22, 1947.
  15. National anthem: January 24, 1950
  16. National song: January 24, 1950
  17. Indian Independence Act 1947
  18. Assembly was fully Sovereign
  19. Dual functions by Assembly
    1. Making of Constitutions: presided by Dr. Rajendra Prasad
    2. Legislative Functions: G V Mavlankar
  20. The Constitution as adopted on November 26, 1949, contained a Preamble, 395 Articles and 8 Schedules
  21. The Preamble was enacted after the entire Constitution was already enacted.
  22. 26 jan 1930 Purna Swaraj day was celebrated, following the resolution of the Lahore Session (December 1929) of the INC.
  23. IMPORTANT FACTS
  24. Elephant: Symbol (seal)
  25. Sir B.N. Rau Legal advisor
  26. H.V.R. Iyengar was the Secretary
  27. S.N. Mukerjee was the chief draftsman
