Chapter 32 State Council of Ministers 

Article 163
  1. Council of ministers to aid and advise governor
  2. Governor enjoys larger discretion than president.
  3. his actions cannot be called into any court on grounds he ought or ought not to have acted in his discretion
Article 164
  1. cm appointed by governor and other minister whose name recommended by cm
  2. also in states of chhattisgarh, jharkhand, mp, orissa shall be a minister in charge of tribal welfare and sc.
  3. Bihar was removed by 94th Act.
  4. council cannot be more than 15% and not less than 12 members
  5. collective responsibility of council
Article 166
  1. governor makes rules of convenient transaction of govt
Article 167
  1. Duty of cm to furnish information to governor
Article 177
  1. every minister shall have right to speak and take part in proceedings of any committee or house. but not entitled to vote.
Nature of Advice by Ministers
  1. council aid and advice governor except on discretionary ones
  2. personal satisfaction is of council not of governor.

Appointments
  1. Tribal welfare minister in Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh and Odisha
  2. 94th Act freed Bihar from obligation to have a tribal minister

No legal Responsibility of ministers as laws are not counter signed by them.
