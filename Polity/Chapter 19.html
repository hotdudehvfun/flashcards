Chapter 19 Prime Minister 
PM real head of government
Appointed by President
1. If no majority is formed, president appoints head of largest party or coalition govt and asks him to prove confidence in one month
2. President can also use discretion when pm dies and no obvious successor
3. A person who is not a member of house can also be elected as PM, but has to get elected in 6 month
4. he may prove his confidence after getting appointed as PM
5.pm can be of any house, but in britain pm has to be from lower house

OATH, TERM AND SALARY 
1. oath of office and secrecy under President
2. pm serves under pleasure of president. but can only be removed of he fails to prove confidence. either he can resign or president can dismiss him

POWERS of PM
1. president appoints only those who gets recommended by PM
2. manage portfolios
3. can ask resignation or advise president to dismiss
4. if he resigns/death, government collapses, council gets dissolved
5. communication channel between council and president
6. pm advises important appointments like CAG, upsc etc
7. pm advises president to summon/prorogue sessions of parliament
8. can recommend dissolution of lower house
9. announces policies on floor
10. chairman of NITI ayog, NDC, National Integration Council, Inter-State Council and National Water Resources Council

ARTICLE 74
there shall be a council headed by PM to aid and advise president.
note: president can ask council to reconsider advice

ARTICLE 75
pm appointed by president
ministers hold office during pleasure of president
council collectively responsible
