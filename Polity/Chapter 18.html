Chapter 18 Vice President 
Vice president 2nd highest rank
Taken from USA

Election
1. Only elected and nominated members of parliament
2. Election disputes handles by SC and its decision is final

Qualifications
1. citizen of india
2. not less than 35 age
3. fit to be member of rajya sabha
4. no office of profit
Must be supported by 50 primary and 50 secondary 
15,000 rupees deposit
Oath administered by President or some person on his behalf

Removal
1. 5 year tenure expires
2. Removal by resolution of rajya sabha passed by absolute majority and also approved by lok sabha.
3. removal requires 14 days notice.
4.  no ground has been mentioned in the Constitution for his removal as of president mentioned



VACANCY IN OFFICE
1. expiry of of five years
2. resignation
3. removal
4. By his death
5. election declared void

POWERS AND FUNCTIONS 
1. chairman of rajya sabha
2. he can only act as a president when office gets vacant due to death, resign, removal only. if term has expired, President holds office until successor comes
3. when vp becomes president, deputy chairman of rajya sabha becomes vp

INDIAN AND AMERICAN VICE-PRESIDENTS COMPARED 
1. American vice president becomes president automatically when office is vacant and holds office for unexpired term
2. in india vp merely act as a president until next comes and does not hold office for unexpired term
