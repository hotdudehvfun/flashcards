Predecessors of INC
1. 1837: Landholders Society
an association of landlords of bengal, bihar, orissa founded in 1837
to promote class interests of landlords

2. 1843: Bengal British Indian Society
to protect and promote general public interest.

3. 1851: above two merged to form British India Association

4. 1852: Madras Native Association, Bombay Association

5. Scientific Society by Sayyid Ahmad Khan
