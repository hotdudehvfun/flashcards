Post War 
Dual System of Govt. in Bengal
  1. Clive started dual govt system in bengal after war
  2. EIC got control over defence, Nawab dependent on EIC
  3. EIC collects taxes and revenue, 
  4. Appointed Deputy Subedar in court, to control Nizaman, police and judicial powers
  5. Same person acted as Deputy Diwan on behalf of EIC, Deputy Subedar on behalf of Nawab: This is Double Govt.
  6. EIC got both powers and no responsibility
  7. Responsibility held with Nawabs
