Union Public Service Commission Article:315-323
Consists of Chairman and others appointed by President
Strength and Composition by President
No specific qualifications except 50% members having 10 years experience in central government
Service condition by President
6 years or 65 age
Removal
Under circumstances:
    1. Bankrupt
    2. Engages in paid employment
    3. Unfit to continue
'Misbehaviour'
    Into agreement or getting profit by agreement
President can consult SC advise.
Advice is binding in NATURE
 Independence
Tenure not pleasure by president
Debarred from further employment
Not eligible for Second Tenure as Chairman
But eligible as   member in UPSC or SPSC
Salaries charged from Consolidated Fund means cannot be put to VOTE
FUNCTIONS
Conducts examinations for appointments
Assists the states (if requested)
Serves all or any of the needs of a state on the request of the state governor and with the approval of the president of India.
Suitability of candidates for appointments, promotions and transfers.
Disciplinary matters 
Supreme Court said if the government fails to consult UPSC aggrieved public servant has no remedy in a court.
Additional functions can be by the Parliament.
UPSC presents report to the president.
President places both the Houses of Parliament
An individual ministry or department has no power to reject the advice of the UPSC. 
 LIMITATIONS
Not in Reservation, SC and ST, Temporary Appointment
Training, cadre, pay and service condition
President can exclude services, posts and matters form purview 
President can make rules and regulations without consulting UPSC
But has to present before both the houses in 14 days. Parliament can amend and repeal
