Advocate General of the State Article: 165
 Highest law officer in the state
Appointed by Governor
Qualified to be Judge of High Court
For High Court Judge: Must be a citizen of India and must have held a judicial office for ten years or been an advocate of a high court for ten years
 Term of office of the advocate general is not fixed by the Constitution.
Constitution does not contain the procedure and grounds for his removal. 
Holds office during the pleasure of the governor
Remuneration as the governor may determine
Perform duties:
Advice on legal matters
Duties  given by governor
Duties conferred by constitution
