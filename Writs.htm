Prohibition

a.      To forbid

b.      Higher court to a lower court or tribunal to prevent the latter from exceeding its jurisdiction or usurping a jurisdiction that it does not possess.

c.      Mandamus that directs activity, the prohibition directs inactivity

d.      Only against judicial and quasi-judicial

e.      NOT issued

                            v.     Against administrative authorities

                          vi.     legislative bodies

                         vii.     private individuals or bodies

a.      to be certified or to be informed

c.      both preventive as well as curative

d.      Against

                             i.     judicial and quasi-judicial

e.      Not Issued
